Fried Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

Step Instruction
1 In a large bowl, soak the chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 2 hours (or overnight).
2 In another bowl, mix the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
3 Heat oil in a deep skillet or fryer over medium-high heat.
4 Remove the chicken from the buttermilk, letting the excess drip off, then dredge in the flour mixture until well coated.
5 Carefully place the coated chicken in the hot oil and fry until golden brown and cooked through (about 15-20 minutes).
6 Remove the chicken and place it on paper towels to drain excess oil.
7 Serve hot with your favorite sides!
