Thai Tea Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tablespoons Thai tea leaves
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

Step Instruction
1 Boil the water in a pot.
2 Add the Thai tea leaves to the boiling water and let steep for 5-10 minutes, depending on desired strength.
3 Strain the tea to remove the leaves.
4 Add sugar to the hot tea and stir until dissolved.
5 Allow the tea to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate.
6 To serve, fill a glass with ice cubes and pour in the chilled tea.
7 Add a splash of condensed milk and evaporated milk on top. Stir to combine and enjoy!
